Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice
  • Eggs
  • Vegetables
  • Soy sauce

Steps

  1. Scramble eggs and set aside.
  2. Stir-fry veggies.
  3. Add rice, soy sauce, and eggs; stir well.
