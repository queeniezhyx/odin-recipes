Hotpot

Description

A social meal where ingredients are cooked in a simmering broth at the table.

Ingredients

  • Hotpot broth base
  • Sliced meats
  • Vegetables
  • Tofu
  • Noodles

Steps

  1. Boil the broth base.
  2. Add and cook ingredients at the table.
  3. Enjoy with dipping sauces.
